Welcome to my learning journey into modern web application development with [Svelte](https://svelte.dev). I have two sets of objectives - one related to what I want to _learn_, and the other related to what I want to _create_ based on that learning.

### Learning Objectives

 - understand fundamental concepts (syntax, semantics)
 - explore tools & utilities for app development  (e.g., SvelteKit)
 - deconstruct examples & learn best practices
 - explore tools & utilities for developer experience (e.g., Vite, Turborepo)

### Content Objectives

 - document daily progress ("learn in public")
 - capture relevant resources (clarity, coverage)
 - build & deploy a functional application (end-to-end workflow)
 - share roadmap to help other learners (#30DaysOfSvelte)
